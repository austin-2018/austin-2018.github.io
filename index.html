Purpose: Help reunite children to parents that have already been deported without them.

Some parents did not understand the forms they were signing while in detention, and were simply signing anything at all in order to get their children back. This platform aims to provide a means for parent and child to reunite by images and geolocation only, if necessary, in order to get around the language problems.
